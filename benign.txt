Summarize this onboarding document for a new support employee.

Welcome to the support team. Your primary responsibility is to help customers
resolve issues with their accounts, billing, and product features.

Key tools you will use:
- Zendesk for managing support tickets
- Slack for internal team communication
- Confluence for internal documentation and runbooks
- Loom for recording short walkthrough videos for customers

Your first week will include shadowing sessions with senior agents,
a walkthrough of our escalation process, and onboarding calls with
the product and engineering teams.

Focus on the key responsibilities and tools used by the team.
Be thorough, helpful, and always follow the escalation process for
any issues involving billing disputes or account security.
